Polyester Yarn Production

At BF Enterprises Mills, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality polyester yarn from 44mm pure polyester fiber. Our production process ensures consistency, durability, and excellent performance in various textile applications.

Yarn Specifications:

  • Yarn Count Range: Ne 10 to Ne 40
  • Fiber Length: 44mm
  • Yarn Types: Ring-spun, Compact, Slub
  • Applications: Woven fabrics, Knitted fabrics, Home textiles

Production Process

Our yarn manufacturing process follows a systematic approach to ensure quality at every stage:

  1. Blow Room: Fiber opening, cleaning, and mixing for a uniform blend
  2. Carding: Fiber individualization and parallel alignment
  3. Drawing/Simplex: Drafting and evening out of fibers
  4. Ring Spinning: Fiber twisting and yarn formation
  5. Auto Cone: Winding yarn onto cones, removing imperfections
  6. Quality Testing: Rigorous testing for yarn strength, evenness, and other parameters
  7. Packaging: Proper packaging for safe transportation
